Culture Sector

Fostering cross-cultural dialogues and rich heritage exchanges.

Overview

We celebrate heritage and diversity through structured cultural exchange programs between Austria and India.

Strategic Partnerships

Collaborating with Ministry of Culture (India), Federal Ministry for Arts & Culture (Austria), and numerous cultural foundations.

Objectives

  • 🔸 Organize annual bilateral cultural festivals in Vienna and New Delhi.
  • 🔸 Promote joint artist-in-residence programs.
  • 🔸 Preserve and digitize heritage links between the two regions.

Key Programs

  • Indo-Austrian Cultural Week
  • Classical Music Exchange Fellowship
