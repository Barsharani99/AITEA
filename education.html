Education Sector

Fostering academic cooperation and education partnerships between Austria and India.

Overview

We promote joint university research, dual degree pathways, and the integration of the Austrian 'Dual System' of vocational training to support skill development.

Strategic Partnerships

Partnering with leading Austrian Universities of Applied Sciences and Indian IITs/Universities.

Objectives

  • 🔸 Facilitate university-to-university MoUs.
  • 🔸 Align vocational training curricula for skilled labor mobility.
  • 🔸 Support scholarships for higher studies.

Key Programs

  • Bilateral University Research Fund
  • Austrian Dual Vocational Pilot in India
