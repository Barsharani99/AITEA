The Founders of AITEA

Meet the visionary leadership steering AITEA's strategic mission.

Co-Founder & Board Member

Gerd Saalfrank

Gerd Saalfrank is an accomplished entrepreneur and strategic consultant with over 25 years of experience in international trade, corporate strategy, and cross-border project management. Having worked extensively in both Central Europe and Southern Asia, he brings a deep understanding of market integration and institutional networks.

Founder's Message

Our goal is simple yet profound: to build a permanent, reliable pipeline of trade, education, and mutual growth between Austria and India. By creating AITEA, we are offering public and private entities a formal, values-based platform to explore and execute collaborative initiatives.

Co-Founder & Advisory Chairperson

Erwin Rader

Erwin Rader is a distinguished educationalist and business adviser based in Vienna. He has designed several high-impact training curricula and corporate partnership frameworks. His passion is leveraging Austria's specialized vocational education systems to foster talent development in India.

Strategic Vision

AITEA represents a vehicle for human capital development. By combining Austrian educational standards with India's immense human talent pool, we create a win-win scenario that benefits businesses, universities, and communities in both nations.
