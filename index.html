Who We Are

The Austrian-Indian Talent Exchange Association (AITEA) represents a pioneering platform dedicated to establishing strong, bilateral linkages between Austria and India. Driven by European values and strategic global ambitions, we unite founders, industry leaders, educational institutes, and cultural ambassadors to create mutual prosperity.
